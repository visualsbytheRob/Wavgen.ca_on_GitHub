Sans Serif - Top four

Open Sans (Sans-Serif)

Open Sans is a clean and modern sans-serif typeface. It has been optimized for print, web, and mobile interfaces, providing excellent readability.

Poppins (Sans-Serif)

Poppins is a geometric sans-serif typeface. Its clean design is ideal for web projects, offering both beauty and legibility.

Source Sans Pro (Sans-Serif)

Source Sans Pro is Adobe's first open-source font. It's highly readable and works well for both body text and headings.

Work Sans (Sans-Serif)

Work Sans is optimized for on-screen use and is highly legible. It strikes a balance between elegance and functionality.




Serif

Libre Baskerville (Serif)

Libre Baskerville is a serif font optimized for body text and long-form reading on screens. It evokes a classic, traditional style, making it suitable for literature and formal documents.

Merriweather (Serif)

Merriweather is a serif typeface with a very large x-height, making it great for reading on screens. It was designed to be comfortable for reading at small sizes.

Playfair Display (Serif)

Playfair Display is a serif typeface that evokes a classic, elegant style. It's often used for large text, such as titles or quotes.

Sans Serif - Other options

Roboto (Sans-Serif)

Roboto has a mechanical skeleton and the forms are largely geometric. It has a friendly and open curve structure that makes it highly readable.

Lato (Sans-Serif)

Lato is a sans-serif typeface family designed in the summer of 2010. Its semi-rounded details give it a feeling of warmth.

PT Sans (Sans-Serif)

PT Sans is based on Russian sans-serif types and has a contemporary appearance. It's neutral yet distinctive in its forms.

Montserrat (Sans-Serif)

Montserrat is a geometric sans-serif typeface. Its letters are bold and distinct, suitable for titles and headers.

Raleway (Sans-Serif)

Raleway is an elegant sans-serif typeface family. It is best suited for headings and larger text sizes.

Ubuntu (Sans-Serif)

The Ubuntu typeface is inspired by the brand of the same name. It has a strong and modern design that makes it ideal for headings and body text.

Oswald (Sans-Serif)

Oswald is a modern sans-serif typeface designed for web use. It's condensed and works great for titles and headlines.

Fira Sans (Sans-Serif)

Fira Sans is a clean sans-serif font optimized for screen readability, ideal for web and app interfaces.

Nunito (Sans-Serif)

Nunito is a well-balanced sans-serif typeface.


Monospace

Inconsolata (Monospace)

Inconsolata is a monospace font designed for code snippets and developer tools. Its clean and clear design ensures legibility even at small sizes.
