Visual Innovation

Digital video technology represents the convergence of art, technology, and human expression. From real-time generative graphics to projection mapping on physical objects, each project explores new possibilities in visual storytelling and immersive experience design.

Working with tools like TouchDesigner and Unreal Engine, I create responsive visual systems that react to data, sound, and user interaction. These technologies enable the creation of dynamic environments that blur the boundaries between digital and physical reality.

Whether designing for live performance, architectural installation, or interactive media, the goal is always to create meaningful visual experiences that engage audiences and push the boundaries of what's possible with digital video technology.
