About The Waveform Generation

The Waveform Generation represents a creative exploration at the intersection of music, visual art, and technology. Based in Toronto, I seek to create immersive experiences that blend traditional artistry with cutting-edge tools.

My work spans electronic music production, real-time graphics, projection mapping, and AI-assisted development. Through this multidisciplinary approach, I explore the creative possibilities of emerging technologies while honoring the roots of electronic music and digital art.

Whether working with TouchDesigner for live visuals, crafting electronic soundscapes, or developing interactive applications, each project aims to push boundaries and create meaningful connections between technology and human expression.
