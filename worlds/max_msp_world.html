COMMUNITY
Artists & Practitioners
Eight figures who shaped the Max/MSP ecosystem
Robert Henke
MONOLAKE
Co-creator of Ableton Live. Built Granulator II in Max for Live. CBL laser installation pushes Max into physical space.
Miller Puckette
THE PROGENITOR
Created Max at IRCAM in 1988, then Pure Data. His work defines the paradigm of visual dataflow programming for music.
Aphex Twin
AFX / RDJ
Developed bespoke algorithmic Max tools for abstract electronic music. Synonymous with pushing software to extremes.
Alva Noto
CARSTEN NICOLAI
Minimalist laptop + Jitter visual synthesis. The "Prototypes" series merges microscopic sound with geometric visuals.
Memo Akten
MEMO
Jitter/AI generative work. "Learning to See" uses convolutional networks in a Max pipeline to reimagine visual perception.
Philippe Manoury
IRCAM PIONEER
"Jupiter" (1987) was the first live electronics concert use of Max — a flute following a live score generated in real time.
Laetitia Sonami
LADY'S GLOVE
The "Lady's Glove" wearable sensor instrument processes hand gestures through Max to generate sound and visuals.
Kyle McDonald
OPENFRAMEWORKS CROSSOVER
ofx/Max crossover artist. Facial recognition social systems art — "People Staring at Computers" challenged surveillance norms.
